Friedutch Plus

About

Friedutch Plus is my self-hosted website for the tools and pages that support my own setup. It brings together a small set of focused projects in one place instead of splitting them across separate apps or dashboards.

Some parts of the site are public and meant to be viewed directly, like the Minecraft page and general project overview. Other parts are private operational tools that are only available to me after Smart Lock verifies my session.

In practice, that means this site works as both a front door and a control surface: public pages explain what is available, while protected pages handle admin actions, file access, and other personal management tasks behind authentication.
